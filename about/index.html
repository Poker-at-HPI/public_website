About

Almost a year ago we started with the organization and development of the HPI Poker Bot Competition as a side project.

Inspired by the MIT Pokerbots event we wanted to create a similar competition at HPI... This year we are proud to present the first edition of the HPI Poker Bot Competition!

What to expect

A hackathon-style competition in which you get to showcase your coding and strategy skills hosted at HPI in Potsdam!

Open to everyone starting on January 10th and ending on January 17th 2025. Prior poker knowledge unnecessary.

Simply implement your poker bot as a team, and win BIG with exclusive prizes and the ultimate bragging rights!

FAQ

Which skills do I need?

No poker knowledge is required! At least one person in your team should have basic programming skills. Our team will give you an introduction into poker and bot programming.

When is the event?

The event takes place from January 10th until January 17th 2025. A more specific schedule coming soon...

How will the event be structured?

We are planning a kickoff and a final competition. Throughout the week there might be a few events but you will have free time to implement your bots.

How can I participate?

Participation is free! There are no participation requirements. You can participate online or in person.

What can I win?

We will host competitions where you can let your poker bot play against others in a tournament. Everyone will have the chance to win cool prizes!

Which variant of poker will be played?

This will be published at the event to allow a fair competition.

In which programming language will I work?

You can implement your poker bot in Python, C++ or Java in our predefined poker bot framework.

Can I work in a team?

You can work in teams of 2 or work alone. We can help you find a teammate if you contact us.

Any hardware requirements?

Having a laptop is useful but you can also work at home from your PC. We will provide hardware for you to run your bots on.
